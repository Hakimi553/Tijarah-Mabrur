About TMSB

Tijarah Mabrur (M) Sdn Bhd is a Malaysian engineering and consultancy firm specializing in industrial safety, machinery compliance, and technical services. Established in 2011, the company brings over 15 years of experience in factory and machinery operations, with a strong focus on compliance with the Department of Occupational Safety and Health (DOSH) regulations across Malaysia.

SERVICES

Consultancy and Compliance

Help businesses follow DOSH rules and meet industry standards.

Training Services

Provide certified training in safety, leadership, and IT, including custom in-house programs.

Fabrication and Repair Services

Build and repair structures, tanks, piping, and assist with machinery inspection and certification.

Robotic Customization & AI Solutions

Combine AI and robotics to improve industrial process accuracy and efficiency.

Engineering and Project Management

Manage and deliver engineering projects from start to finish, especially in oil and gas sectors.

Cleaning Services

Offer advanced cleaning methods like sandblasting, dry ice, and high-pressure cleaning.
